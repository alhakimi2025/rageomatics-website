Why GIS Matters for Engineering Projects

2026-03-12 • GIS, ArcGIS Pro

GIS helps teams understand location context, analyze risk, and connect field data with engineering deliverables. In road design, hydrology, and planning, GIS reduces errors and improves decision-making.

Practical examples

  • Watershed and flow-path analysis using DEM.
  • High-resolution basemaps before design.
  • Tracking urban change over time.
